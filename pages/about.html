About

LAWCATOR provide a user-friendly platform to help individuals find legal services that match their specific needs. This project is a demonstration of how technology can make legal resources more accessible,transparent and approachable for anyone. Below, you can learn more about the features we offer, the data we use, and our plans for future development.

Project Highlights

What does LAWCATOR provide?

A clean overview of firms, services, and lawyer profiles, plus search tools that make it easy to explore options.

Where does the data come from?

All listings are dummy data prepared for coursework and demonstrations. Nothing shown represents real legal services.

What is planned next?

Refinements to filtering, map interactions, and more detailed firm profiles to improve the browsing experience.
